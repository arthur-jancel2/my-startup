MediRecycle

Extend the life of medicines and combat shortages

About Us

MediRecycle is a pre-seed startup, founded by Arthur Jancel, a student at HEC Paris. It focuses on improving medicines' accessibility and creating solutions that ensure the reuse of medicines while reducing waste.

Our Concept

MediRecycle is a system between private individuals and pharmacies designed to extend the life of medicines and combat shortages. Individuals can exchange medicines they don't need in pharmacies. Thanks to a home delivery system, MediRecycle guarantees access to low-cost care for the elderly and disabled, while respecting health standards.
